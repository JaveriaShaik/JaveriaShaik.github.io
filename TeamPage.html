Our Team

Meet the members of Our Team!

Pikachu, CEO

Coming from the Kanto region, Pikachu is battle companion that has evolved into a symbol of high-voltage energy. He's known for his signature 'Pika-Pika' and quick reactions. As our CEO, Pikachu leads our company with sharp decision making and strong determination. He is loyal and a fierce stragegist that leads the team to push through boundaries daily. He works with each member of the Team, talking about concerns and goals, guiding them through challenges. Pikachu’s quick thinking and positive charge keep the Team moving forward. Under his leadership, no challenge is too shocking to handle.

Doraemon, Director of research and Development

Doraemon is a 22nd century cat robot who traveled back in time to support a struggling boy, Nobita Nobi. He has advanced gadgets and a genius level understanding of technology. He's calm, analytical, and loyal. Now, he is serving as the Director of Research and Technology (R&D Director), contributing in developing new gadget that help us advance. Whether it’s automating repetitive tasks or developing new tools for collaboration, he’s always one step ahead. He also provides on-demand gadget assistance that he stores in his 3 dimensional pocket.

Shinchan Nohara, Internal Communications and consultant

Shinnosuke Nohara, widely known as Shinchan is from Japan. He has a mature sense of humor and a talent for creating the environment lively. He's quick witted, unique, and fearless which makes him a viral sensation. As a Internal Communications Consultant, Shinchan crafts weekly newsletters, organizes important events, and even helps out HR announcements. He works hard to make everyone feel stress free and smile high. He is also respinsible for consulting with clients, making them understand the importance and interested in working together.

SpongeBob SquarePants, Chief of Human Resources

SpongeBob was born and raised in Bikini Bottom. He is known for his optimism, kindness, his ability to arrange events in a fun and engaging way. He has gained deeper understanding of workplace dynamics after managing grumpy coworkers like Squidward. Today, as the Chief of Human Resources, SpongeBob ensures that every employee feels valued and appreciated. He holds events for team building and morale boosting which helps the workers work in a more relaxed environment. With SpongeBob at the helm, our HR department runs on empathy, fun, and jellyfish jelly sandwiches.
